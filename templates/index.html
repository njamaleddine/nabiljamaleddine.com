Who am I?

I'm a fullstack developer currently employed at the design and development consultancy JAKT. I spend most of my time developing in Django, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, LESS, and HTML5 while being mindful of the philosophy "Explicit is better than implicit." Although most of my work is server side, I embrace all parts of the development process.

When I'm not developing web applications, building APIs, and architechting databases, I enjoy browsing hacker news and reddit in an effort to keep up with some of the latest news on software and technology.

A strong, frothy cup of coffee is my most important development tool.
